About Singh HR Private Limited

Singh HR Private Limited

Founded: 2022 | Headquarters: Aurangabad, Maharashtra | Industry: Educational Technology & HR Solutions

Quickmark is a flagship product of Singh HR Private Limited, a leading technology company committed to delivering innovative HR and attendance solutions for modern organizations and educational institutions across India and beyond.

With over 3 years of experience in human resource management and educational technology, we understand the unique challenges faced by institutions in managing attendance and student engagement. Our mission is to revolutionize education through cutting-edge technology solutions that enhance learning outcomes and administrative efficiency.

Founded by visionary entrepreneur Abhishek Singh in 2022, Singh HR Private Limited has grown from a small startup to a trusted partner for educational institutions across India. We specialize in developing comprehensive solutions that address the evolving needs of modern education systems, with a focus on AI-powered technologies and user-friendly interfaces.
